Frequently Asked Questions

What is eBidding?

eBidding is an online auction system where buyers can bid on products or services, competing with other users to win the auction.

How do I place a bid?

You can place a bid by clicking the 'Place Bid' button next to the item you're interested in. Enter your bid amount and confirm your bid.

How do I know if my bid is successful?

You will receive a notification if you win an auction. You can also check your bidding status in your account dashboard.

What are the payment options available?

We accept various payment methods such as credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose your preferred option during checkout.

Can I cancel my bid?

Bids cannot be canceled once placed. Please double-check your bid amount and ensure you want to bid before confirming.
